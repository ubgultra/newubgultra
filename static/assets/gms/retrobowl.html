Welcome to Retro Bowl

Retro Bowl is a popular American football video game that combines simple, retro-style graphics with addictive gameplay. Players take on the role of a football team's coach and general manager, making decisions on player personnel, game strategy, and in-game play calls. The game features a unique blend of management and simulation elements, allowing players to balance team morale, player skills, and fan expectations while competing against other teams.
