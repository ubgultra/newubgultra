Welcome to Five Night's At Freddy's 2

Get ready to face a new wave of terror as a security guard at the rebuilt Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, where the old animatronics have been joined by a new cast of deadly characters, including the Marionette and the twisted Toy animatronics. With new security systems and a fresh coat of paint, the restaurant may look safer, but the horrors that lurk in the shadows are more sinister than ever. Can you survive the night shift and uncover the dark secrets behind the restaurant's gruesome past? Happy gaming!
