Welcome to Five Night's At Freddy's 3

Get ready to confront the darkest corners of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza's twisted legacy as a security guard at Fazbear's Fright: The Attraction, a haunted theme park built on top of the old restaurant's ruins. The park's owners have attempted to capitalize on the restaurant's dark history, but the animatronics - including the vengeful Springtrap - have other plans. With a new set of security systems and a desperate bid to survive, can you uncover the truth behind the restaurant's horrible past and make it through the night? Happy gaming!
